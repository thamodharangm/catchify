/* DESCRIPTION */

Catchify is a free Android app for streaming and downloading music. It has
no ads, no subscriptions, and includes offline playback, custom playlists,
an equalizer, lyrics, and support for 21+ languages.

/* FEATURES */

- No ads, no subscriptions, no hidden costs
- Offline downloads for songs and playlists
- Custom playlists with import via link and folder organization
- Adjustable equalizer with ready-made presets
- Synced lyrics
- 21+ language interface with curated regional suggestions
- Time Machine: monthly listening recap
- SponsorBlock: automatically skips sponsored segments
