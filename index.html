chat_bubble Frequently Asked Questions

What is Catchify?

Catchify is a free Android app for streaming and downloading music, with no ads, no subscriptions, offline playback, custom playlists, an equalizer, lyrics, and support for 21+ languages including Tamil, Hindi, and Telugu.

Is Catchify free to use?

Yes. Catchify is completely free — no ads, no subscription plans, and no hidden costs. You can use it forever for free.

Is Catchify a good Spotify alternative?

Yes. Catchify is a free, ad-free alternative to Spotify for Android. Unlike Spotify, there are no subscription fees, no ads interrupting your music, and you can download unlimited songs offline at no cost.

Is Catchify a good JioSaavn / Gaana alternative?

Yes. Catchify is completely free with no ads — a great replacement for JioSaavn, Gaana, or Wynk Music, especially for Indian regional language users. It supports Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, and 18+ more languages with curated music suggestions.

What is the best free music app for Android without ads?

Catchify is one of the best free, ad-free music apps for Android. It's open-source, requires no subscription, and offers offline downloads, equalizer, lyrics, and 21+ language support — making it a top choice among free music apps.

Can I listen to music offline with Catchify?

Yes. Download songs and full playlists for offline listening — no internet needed after download.

What languages does Catchify support?

The interface supports 21+ languages, with curated music suggestions for Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, Marathi, Bengali, Kannada, Punjabi, Malayalam, Gujarati, Urdu, Odia, Assamese, Sanskrit, and Konkani.

Is Catchify open source?

Yes. Catchify is fully open-source. The source code is available on GitHub.

How do I install Catchify?

Tap the Download button on this page — the APK downloads directly. Open it on your Android device and allow installation from unknown sources if prompted.

Who made Catchify?

Catchify is developed by Thamodharan Ganesan, an independent developer.
